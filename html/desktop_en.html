        Welcome Tour

        1. Welcome!

          With Intranet Lite you can share, collaborate and find the information you want right in your own website.

        2. Know where you are on the map

          Context is everything and the context map shows all your workspaces. Intranet Lite is designed with ten types of workspaces.

        3. Pick your preferences

          Enable notifications for a page using Options to subscribe, bookmark or tag pages to appear in the left menu.

        4. Start a new page

          Communication happens in workspaces. Start a new page from the list. Send direct messages using Take a look. Assign someone to do IT support, Facility support or an action item.

        6. Setup your team

          Send invitations to people when ready. They will get a link to the account to create a password and sign in.

        Welcome Tour

          Welcome!

          With Intranet Lite you can share, collaborate and find the information you want right in your own website.

        1. Know where you are on the map

          Context is everything and the context map shows all your workspaces. Intranet Lite is designed with ten types of workspaces.

        2. Pick your preferences

          Enable notifications for a page using Options to subscribe, bookmark or tag pages to appear in the left menu.

        3. Start a new page

          Communication happens in workspaces. Start a new page from the list. Send direct messages using Take a look. Assign someone to do IT support, Facility support or an action item.

        4. See your stuff

          Workspaces are shared with you. See recent activities, updates on discussions, messages and notifications.

        Creating Help Tickets

        1. Help Tickets

          Help Tickets automate workflow for IT and Facility help requests and puts your intranet to work for you by creating a simple assignment between two people. Tasks are open until you close the workflow to show it is complete.

        2. Create a Help Ticket

          Hover or touch the Add New button. Select IT Help Ticket or Facility Help Ticket from the list and then enter a title for the Help Ticket. Click Create Draft to start the process.

        Submitting Help Tickets

        1. Edit the Ticket

          The help ticket includes three sections to capture text, discussions and upload files. Once you enter the information for the help ticket you can route it to any user.

        2. Assign the Ticket

          When you are ready to assign the ticket, click Route To in the workflow menu or on the right side of the Page Actions toolbar. Select a user and click Send. The selected team member will receive an email notifying them of the assignment. When they are finished, they will route it back to you so you can close the ticket.

        Announcements

        1. Announcements

          Use the BPMContext intranet plugin for securely publishing team communications, announcements and news only to your team members.

        2. Create an Announcement

          Hover or touch the Add New button. Select Announcement from the list and then enter a headline for the announcement. Click Create Draft to start the process.

        Publishing Announcements

        1. Edit Announcement

          The Announcement includes three sections to capture text, discussions and upload files. Once you enter the information for the Announcement you can publish it in your intranet.

        2. Publish the Announcement

          When you are ready to publish the Announcement, click Publish in the workflow menu or on the right side of the Page Actions toolbar. Subscribing team members will receive an email notifying them of the new document and the document will become visible to all team members.

        Adding Users

        1. Intranet Users are Free

          As an admin you can add team members to the account using the Account Manager. The Account Manager is visible to Admins only.

        2. Send Invitations

          You can invite members of your team by email. Send invitations from the account manager.

        3. User Status

          You can also see who accepted their invitation and is active.
